Dosa

A DOSA is a thin pancake or crepe originating from South India ,made from a fermented batter predomintiantly consisng of lentils and rice .It is somewhat similar to a crepe in appearence, although savoury flavours are generally emphasized.It was first originated in India. there are several variations of dosa flavours such as ;masala dosa ,rava dosa ghee roast dosa, cheese dosa ,neer dosa and many more .Dosa is a Typical South Indian staple .It is served along Sambhar ( a tangy lentil soup .)

  • ingredients
  • black gram lentils
  • rice
  • mashed potatoes for stuffing

  1. recipe
  2. add rice and lentils in a container wash them and fill them with water and leave overnight.
  3. next day , drain all water and blend the mixture un a jar .
  4. prepare potato stuffing with boiled potatoes and oninions.
  5. pore some ghee over pan , and spread the prepared batter on it like a sheet.
  6. lift the dosa up from ends and roll it and serve it with the stuffing prepared.
